About

The College of Education in Morong is part of the University of Rizal System (URS), offering BEEd, BSEd, BTLED, and BTVTED programs, along with graduate studies. Known for excellent accreditation performance and strong academic programs.

VISION

The leading University in human resource development, knowledge and technology generation and environmental stewardship.

MISSION

The University of Rizal System is committed to nurture and produce upright and competent graduates through relevant and sustainable instruction, research, extension and production services.
